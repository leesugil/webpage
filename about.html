ABOUT SUGIL LEE

Sugil Lee received a Ph.D. in Mathematics at Michigan State University studying algebraic geometry and arithmetic geometry.

Originally from Seoul, Sugil Lee received a B.S. in Statistics and Business from Sunkyunkwan Univeristy and a second B.S. in Mathematics from Stony Brook University. He graduated in Summer 2020 and is currently working on data science and artifical intelligence projects as well as developing methods of technology in education for mathematics learners.
