ABOUT SUGIL LEE

Sugil Lee is a Ph.D. candidate in Mathematics at Michigan State University studying algebraic geometry and arithmetic geometry.

Originally from Seoul, Sugil Lee received a B.S. in Statistics and Business from Sunkyunkwan Univeristy and a second B.S. in Mathematics from Stony Brook University. He is expected to graduate in Summer 2020 and currently working on data science and artifical intelligence projects as well as developing methods of technology in education for mathematics learners.
